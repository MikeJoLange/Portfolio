These tools can be found at garden centers, hardware stores, or online retailers specializing in gardening supplies.

Watering Can

A watering can is an essential tool for watering indoor plants. It allows for precise watering and prevents over-saturation of the soil.

Spray Bottle

A spray bottle is handy for misting indoor plants, especially those that require high humidity. It helps to keep the foliage moist and can also be used to apply foliar fertilizers or insecticides.

Pruning Shears

Pruning shears, also known as secateurs, are used for trimming and shaping plants. They are especially useful for removing dead or diseased foliage and encouraging healthy growth.

Hand Trowel

A hand trowel is a small, handheld tool used for digging, planting, and transplanting. It is perfect for working with potted plants or small garden beds.

Soil Moisture Meter

A soil moisture meter is a handy gadget for checking the moisture level of the soil in potted plants. It helps prevent overwatering or underwatering by providing accurate readings of soil moisture.

Plant Mister

A plant mister is used to spray a fine mist of water onto indoor plants. It helps to increase humidity around the plants and keeps the foliage clean

Plant Labels

Plant labels or markers are used to identify different plants in your indoor garden. They can be made of plastic, metal, or bamboo and are handy for keeping track of plant varieties and care instructions.
